About Me

I am currently an Assistant Professor at IIITDM Kurnool (joined January 2026). Previously, I worked as a Research Scientist at IIT Delhi focusing on LLM evaluation methods for reasoning and climate data analytics.

I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from IIT Kharagpur, where my research focused on multi-dimensional news content analysis, including event extraction, entity and aspect identification, sentiment classification, and media bias analysis using NLP and Information Extraction frameworks.
